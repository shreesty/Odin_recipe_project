MOMO recipe:

Here's a recipe for momos:

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup finely chopped cabbage
  • 1/2 cup chopped scallions
  • 2 tbsp grated ginger
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • tbsp cornstarch
  • Salt and pepper to taste

    • For the dough:

    • 2 cups all-purpose flour
    • 3/4 cup warm water
    • 1/2 tsp salt

    For the filling:

    • 1 lb ground chicken or pork

    Instructions:

    1. Make the dough by mixing the flour, salt, and water in a bowl until it forms a smooth dough. Cover and let rest for 30 minutes.
    2. While the dough is resting, prepare the filling. Mix together the ground meat, cabbage, scallions, ginger, soy sauce, sesame oil, cornstarch, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
    3. Divide the dough into small balls and roll each one out into a thin circle.
    4. Place a spoonful of the filling in the center of each circle.
    5. To shape the momos, bring the edges of the circle together and pinch them closed, forming a pleated edge.
    6. Repeat with the remaining dough and filling.
    7. Steam the momos in a steamer basket for 10-12 minutes, until fully cooked.
    8. Serve hot with your favorite dipping sauce, such as soy sauce or hot sauce.

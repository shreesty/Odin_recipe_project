Keema noodles recipe:

Here's a recipe for Keema noodles:

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 lb ground lamb or beef
  • 8 oz egg noodles
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp ginger, grated
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tbsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/2 tsp ground turmeric
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped cilantro, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook the noodles according to package directions. Once done, drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the oil over medium-high heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until softened, about 3-4 minutes.
  3. Add the garlic and ginger to the skillet and cook for an additional minute.
  4. Add the ground beef or lamb to the skillet and cook until browned and cooked through, breaking it up into small pieces as it cooks.
  5. Add the cumin powder, coriander powder, garam masala, chili powder, and salt to the skillet and stir well.
  6. Add the chopped tomatoes, cilantro, and green chili (if using) to the skillet and stir to combine.
  7. Cook for an additional 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the tomatoes have softened and the mixture has thickened.
  8. Add the cooked noodles to the skillet and toss to combine with the keema mixture.
  9. Add the butter to the skillet and stir until melted and well combined.
  10. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious Keema Noodles!

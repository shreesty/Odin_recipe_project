Coffee recipe:

Here's a recipe for Coffee:

Ingredients:

  • Freshly roasted coffee beans
  • water
  • milk

Instructions:

  1. Measure out the amount of coffee you want to make. A good rule of thumb is to use 1 to 2 tablespoons of coffee for every 6 ounces of water.
  2. Fill the coffee maker with cold, fresh water up to the fill line.
  3. Grind the coffee beans to a medium grind.
  4. Place a filter in the coffee maker basket and add the ground coffee.
  5. Turn on the coffee maker and let it brew until all the water has passed through the coffee and into the carafe.
  6. Turn off the coffee maker and pour the coffee into a mug.
  7. Add milk, sugar, or any other desired flavorings to taste.
